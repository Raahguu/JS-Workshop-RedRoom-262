Signup Form

This form checks to make sure that all of the field within it are valid, before the form is submitted.

  • The name should be between 3 and 40 characters, and must have a space
  • The email must have an '@' and be from a '.com' or '.au' domain
  • The age must be a number of at least 18
  • If all the checks pass, the form says 'Sign Up successful'
  • Otherwise an approriate error message is displayed
